About Me

I have an extensive track record of bringing to all my roles:

  • An extremely high standard of professionalism
  • A driven-to-deliver work ethic
  • A level of performance I take pride in

My Background and Journey

Prior to my career path pivot to becoming a Fullstack Software Developer, I enjoyed working in a variety of positions in a very different professional space.
For over a decade, the roles I performed specialized mainly in the overlapping areas of promoting and protecting Civil and Human Rights. My intersecting activities included:

Developing and Implementing Policy

DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion) policy development and implementation.

Strategic Planning

Developing organizations' strategic plans for various projects and initiatives.

Project Management

Managing projects funded by numerous Government Departments and the European Union.

Academic Research

Conducting academic research and producing publications.

Public Policy Formation

Forming public policy on various social justice issues.

Social Justice Advocacy

Engaging in social justice advocacy and related activities.

Above are examples from my professional background mainly within the Third Sector in Ireland, also known as the Community and Voluntary Sector. The valuable contributions I made carrying out my duties in each of my roles crosscut a wide range of non-profit networks and organizations at the:

  • Local community
  • Regional
  • National
  • European Union
  • and Global levels

Relationships

A key strength I offer that forms perhaps the most important aspect for me of working with anyone in any capacity within every context is taking a person-centred approach to collectively meeting team targets and project goals. One of my highest priorities is always establishing and maintaining a rapport with my colleagues based on mutual understanding, support, trust, and open, positive communication.

I offer an invaluable and intuitive set of transversal or 'soft' skills to build excellent relationships, whether I am working alongside members of my own team, other internal units, or collaborating across multiple departments within the organization. In order to successfully deliver a product and overall positive outcome, it is so important, in my experience, to take the same approach as deemed appropriate when engaging with external stakeholders too. On a one-to-one level, face to face, video call, or having a coffee together, I treat stakeholders involved in once-off short-term projects in the same way I do with a stakeholder that requires an ongoing long-term relationship.

Work Culture

Understanding the ecosystem I am expected to operate in is one of the many fundamental principles of the success I enjoy and satisfaction I gain through the projects I undertake.
In order to deliver when and as needed, I appreciate that I must give equal priority to (a) giving special attention to the specifications of my own responsibilites outlined in my brief and at the same time to (b) the care I show when operating as part of a collective I am contributing to so shared goals are realised.
Many times, I have found myself the closest one to a problem. If I am struggling the most with fixing a bug then that is the moment I reach out to others for their input. This joined up thinking provides the perspective I need to resolve almost any matter.
